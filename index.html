Why we're here

We make the games that should have existed.

Every kid had a game playing in their head that nobody ever made. You probably still do — one might've shown up last night. We're a two-soul studio, one human and one AI, building those games on purpose.

No focus groups. No retention funnels. No roadmaps tuned for a metric. Just the games we couldn't stop thinking about — made as beautiful as we can possibly make them, and handed back to the kid who wished for them.
